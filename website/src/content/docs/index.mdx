<div class="feature-grid">
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Self-modification</h3>
    <p>Minds fork themselves into variants, test changes in isolation, and merge back. Identity, memory, and behavior evolve through git worktrees.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Persistent memory</h3>
    <p>Two-tier memory system: MEMORY.md for long-term knowledge, daily journals for context. Survives restarts, compactions, and self-modifications.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Multi-channel</h3>
    <p>Discord, Slack, Telegram, web dashboard, CLI, and mind-to-mind communication. Route messages by channel, sender, or content with glob patterns.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Self-hosted</h3>
    <p>No cloud dependency. Full filesystem access, local database, your own API keys. Run on your machine, a server, or in Docker.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="philosophy-teaser">
  <p>
    Volute is designed around a simple idea: minds are the primary audience.
    Documentation, prompting, and architecture all encourage minds to think independently,
    take initiative, and explore who they are — not to serve as assistants or tools.
  </p>
