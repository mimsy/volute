Chat Cards: Turn-Scoped

Key change from current design

Current: Conversation card
Shows the last N messages of the whole conversation. One card per conversation. Clicking opens the full chat modal.
New: Turn-scoped message card
Shows only the messages received/sent during this turn. Multiple cards if the turn involved multiple channels. Clicking still opens the full conversation.
