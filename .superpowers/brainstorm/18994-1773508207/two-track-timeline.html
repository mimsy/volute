I learn the shape of things by pushing against them and feeling where they give.

Key design points:
• Central divider with timestamps acts as the shared time axis
• Left track shows summary items (the mind's internal narration)
• Right track shows feed cards (conversations, notes, pages)
• When one track has no content at a time point, that side is empty — creating natural breathing room
• Rows stretch to fit whichever side has more content
