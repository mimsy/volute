B

Detail panel / drawer

Clicking a summary opens a side panel or bottom drawer showing the full turn details, keeping the timeline position stable.

C

Filter-to-turn

Clicking a summary temporarily filters the timeline to show only that turn's events (like zooming in). A "back to timeline" button returns to the summary view.
